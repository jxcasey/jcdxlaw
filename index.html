Citation Quick Reference

Case Law (footnote)
Author v Respondent [Year] Court Volume Reporter StartPage

Legislation (footnote)
Short Title Year (Act No. X of Year) s X

Book (footnote)
Initials Surname Title (Year) PageNo
eg: T Honoré The South African Law of Trusts 5 ed (1992) 45
Journal Article (footnote)
Initials Surname 'Title' (Year) Volume Journal StartPage PageNo
eg: AJ van der Walt 'Property Rights' (2008) 24 SAJHR 28 30
